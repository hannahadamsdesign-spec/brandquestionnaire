Brand Discovery Questionnaire

A comprehensive intake for brand identity, messaging, and creative direction. Your insights here will directly shape the work — every detail helps.

Don't worry about getting the language "perfect" — these responses are a starting point for collaboration, not a final draft. If a question doesn't apply, just skip it.

Section 01

Organization & Context

01

Describe what you do in one to two sentences, as if explaining to someone outside your industry.

02

Not just a formal statement — what drives the work? What would be different in the world if you fully succeed?

03

Stage of business, team size and structure, key milestones or recent changes.

04

What can someone actually use, buy, or engage with today? If still in development, describe what exists now versus what's on the roadmap.

05

Something a non-expert could understand. A real scenario, even if small.

Section 02

Audience & Market

06

07

09

10
